BUS922 A3 · The slice

The Learn slice — one individual act, end to end

The bounded research object: a single learner takes an authentic operational problem and generates an account of it; a designer-built template carries the structuring so the learner only generates; the result is a context-rich intermediate ready for downstream reuse. One generative act produces two things at once — the learning and the artefact. That dual output is the thesis the whole slice turns on.

Raw material · Partner-situated The situated operational knowledge each acquired workforce carries across 200+ offices — client relationships, regulatory jurisdictions, market conditions — a strategic asset that stays invisible without a mechanism to surface it.
↓
DP1 · raw material

Authentic operational problem

Anchor the act in a real problem the learner brings or chooses — not a hypothetical. Authenticity (not difficulty) is what drives transferable learning. Andragogy enters here as the design heuristic.

For the partnerA real freight / regulatory / client case from the learner's own office.
Herrington et al. 2013 · Perusso & Baaken 2022 · Knowles 1975
→
DP2 · generation

Generative articulation

The learner produces a short, prompted explicit account of the problem. Self-explanation drives the learning (g = .55); generation is simultaneously the learning and the externalised artefact.

For the partnerCaptures tacit operational know-how with no dedicated L&D function to elicit it.
Bisra et al. 2018 · Fiorella & Mayer 2016 · Schön 1987
→
DP3 · the template HEADLINE

Structuring lives in the template

A designer-built GenAI template carries the reusable structure — it elicits reflection, auto-applies titles, tags, metadata. The learner only generates. The template fades gently and adaptively as expertise grows.

For the partnerRelocating codification into the template is what makes capture feasible where there is no KM function to do it.
van Merriënboer & Boot 2008 · & Sweller 2010 · Tetzlaff et al. 2025 · Salden et al. 2010
→
DP4 · the output

Context-rich intermediate

Capture a rich, situated intermediate and stop. Reuse-preparation happens later — that's Share's job. Do not decontextualise at the point of creation; the context is the strategic asset.

For the partnerThe seam where the pipeline turns individual know-how into collective, shareable knowledge.
Markus 2001 · Rivière et al. 2019 · Szulanski 2000 · Kasper et al. 2010
→
The dual output · the by-product thesis
generate = learn + artefact
One act, produced once — the learner is more capable, and the organisation holds a reusable record. Not two tasks; the same task.
What the act leaves behind

A shareable record

Context-rich, structured by the template, ready for the next learner and the wider network — without asking the author to do the codifying.
